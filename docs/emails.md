A bit about this project: CHANNELERS has been a slow simmer experimental performance project for a few years now. The core collaborators are myself, composer and performance artist Anna Oxygen, and video/media artist Jeff Larson (who you might have met during the Ignite Festival). For this round we'll also be working with musician/dancer Jane Paik. 
 
In a couple weeks, from June 22-28, we'll be doing a short workshop to continue develop the piece. There's a long backstory to it, but we're thinking a lot about the strange transactions and exchanges we have with AI and LLMs. For this round, the goal is to create a strange DMV-esque void space where visitors can fill out absurdist "intake surveys." That information then becomes the basis for generating music and dance, and it culminates in a weird and witchy ritual where they can select from a menu of AI Oracles (a child, AI on drugs, a tree, etc.) and talk with it to get a "live" divination. 
 
Thus far we've hacked together ways to process visitor information, i.e. manually copy/pasting text form Google forms into different LLM prompts. We also have a working but crude way of doing speech recognition so a visitor can "talk" with our AI Oracles. But for this round we want some slightly more tailored tools to interact with the AI systems.
